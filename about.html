About Le Eden

Le Eden is one of the finest resort cottages in wayanad district of kerala. Located at Sulthan Bathery Le Eden consists of well designed One Bedroom, One Bedroom with balcony and two bedroom balcony. Where all rooms have an outstanding view of forest and typical paddy fields. The misty valley view and renowned wayanad special spicy coffee from each room’s balcony and our tree hut will make your stay unforgettable. Located nearby major tourist point Le Eden is ideal shelter for travelers looking to explore scenic beauty of Wayanad. Muthanga Wildlife sanctuary, a major attraction in wayanad is just 10 minutes drive from our place. All the mind blowing beauty of western ghat can be enjoyed here..

Our villa is located in a breathtaking setting, surrounded by lush greenery, stunning vistas, and natural wonders. Whether it's the majestic mountains, a sparkling river, or a serene forest, you will be captivated by the beauty that surrounds you. Take leisurely walks, embark on nature trails, or simply sit back and immerse yourself in the serenity of the location.

We believe that a memorable vacation should be accessible to everyone. That's why we offer our cottages at an affordable rate, ensuring that you can enjoy a luxurious experience without breaking the bank. Our commitment to providing excellent value for money sets us apart, allowing you to savor a remarkable getaway at an affordable price.
